Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of passionate technologists and has since grown into a global platform for innovation and collaboration. From humble beginnings, it now hosts thousands of attendees each year from across the tech industry.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to inspire, educate, and connect people working on the future of technology. Our mission is to foster a collaborative environment that encourages breakthrough thinking, promotes inclusivity, and accelerates innovation around the world.
